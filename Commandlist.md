# 📘 AI Mini Computer – Full Command List

Below is the complete list of commands supported by the AI Mini Computer.
All commands are entered through the Arduino Serial Monitor.

---

## 🟦 Basic Commands
- help  
- hello  
- hi  
- hey  
- time  
- uptime  
- echo <text>  

---

## 🧮 Math Commands
- add X Y  
- sub X Y  
- what is X+Y  
- what is X-Y  
- what is X*Y  
- what is X/Y  

---

## 🌍 Fact / Knowledge Commands
- ask <question>  
- who is <name>  
- what is <topic>  
- where is <place>  

Examples:
- ask what is the capital of japan  
- who is albert einstein  
- what is the speed of light  

---

## 📈 Plotting Commands (Serial Plotter)
- plot sine  
- plot cosine  
- plot ramp  
- stop  

---

## 🤖 AI Personality Commands
- clanker  
- (any message containing “you”)  
- (any message containing “bored”)  
- (any message containing “joke”)  

---

## Notes
- Commands are not case‑sensitive  
- Extra spaces are ignored  
- AI always responds in normal English  
- Plotting requires Tools → Serial Plotter
